    Ski resorts Montana

    List and map of all 17 ski resorts in Montana

    In Montana, you can look forward to 829 kilometres of slopes: the ski resorts are served by 121 ski lifts. Have lots of fun skiing in Montana!

    Skiing in Montana

    In the list of the best ski resorts in Montana, the ski resort Big Sky Resort is top with 3.9 out of 5 stars. The largest ski resorts offer up to 250 kilometres of slopes (Big Sky Resort). The highest ski resorts for skiing in Montana extend up to an altitude of 3,398 metres (Big Sky Resort). And our tip for the best value for money is the ski resort Maverick Mountain.
